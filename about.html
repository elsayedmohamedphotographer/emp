At EM studios, we're inspired by compelling stories. We enjoy watching your story unfold before our camera lens to eternalize the moment forever. These pictures are the most spontaneous, un-staged ones, having said that, at EM studios, we do everything we can to be there, at the right angle, when the most unexpected moments happen.

Our team of skilled photographers is trained to look for every big and small detail. We like to explore new styles, formats, and setups, in order to strike the perfect visual tone for each client. Since no two stories are ever alike, so we aim to make no two pictures the same.

Elsayed Mohamed , principal photographer, and owner of Em studios, is a self-taught photographer, based in Alexandria, Egypt. Elsayed has over 12 years of experience in the photography industry, working with the biggest studios around Egypt.

he worked in events, fashion, and commercial photography for over 4 years before setting up his own studio in 2021.

expresses his feelings about being included in important occasions saying: “People invite me to be part of the single, most important day of their lives and allow me into their inner circle of trust, which makes me feel more than just a photographer but rather afamily member. For me, photography is more like a time machine capturing moments that are otherwise gone forever.”
